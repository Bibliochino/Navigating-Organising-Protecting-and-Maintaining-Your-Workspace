A 30-minute sample lesson delivered as part of a "Train the Trainer" course (QQI Level 6), designed to equip clerical officers at the Department of Social Protection (DSP), Intreo Centre Galway, with fundamental IT skills to support the department's ongoing digitisation wave.

Key Details:

•	Course: Digital Foundations for Public Service

•	Audience: Clerical officers, DSP & Intreo Centre Galway

•	Objective: Upskill staff in basic IT competencies to adapt to evolving digital processes within the public service

•	Context: Delivered as part of the Training Delivery & Evaluation module (6N3326), demonstrating lesson planning, instructional delivery, and learner engagement strategies
